St. Louie's Community College

Planning Your Future?

A higher education institution welcoming learners of all ages and offering career-ready degrees, transfer paths to 4 year degrees, and continuing education. Established in 1981, St. Louie's (SLCC) was the first community college in Selmer Tennessee and remains the only publicly funded college in Selmer. Its campus is located in the heart of McNairy county with a total enrollment of 2500 students.
